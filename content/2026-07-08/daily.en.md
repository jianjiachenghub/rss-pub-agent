# Personal Daily | July 8, 2026

## Today's Take

> Today's core read is that the AI narrative is shifting from "model launches" to a simultaneous repricing of cost, access, memory, and compliance. OpenAI's expansion of its top model, the evolution of voice capabilities, and Microsoft's use of in-house models to lower office AI costs all show that both product form and business economics are changing. At the same time, Asian AI capital rotating from South Korea to China, and Temasek adding exposure to AI and private credit, show that risk appetite has not exited. It is simply choosing new assets to carry it.
