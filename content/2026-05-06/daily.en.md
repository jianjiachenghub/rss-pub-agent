# Personal Daily | May 6, 2026

## Today's Take

> The market is undergoing dual pricing of 'macro easing + tech acceleration.' The receding geopolitical risk (US-Iran deal expectations) and Fed policy swings are providing a liquidity window for tech stocks, while AI's leap from conversational tools to autonomous agents (Meta) and rigid capex in compute-energy infrastructure signal the industry's entry into a heavy-asset phase. The deeper shift lies in capital structure: state-backed entry into DeepSeek contrasts with Samsung/US chip leaders' market-cap breakthroughs, confirming that the tech race is no longer purely commercial but a sovereign investment imperative.
