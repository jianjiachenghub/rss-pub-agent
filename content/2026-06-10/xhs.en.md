**🚨 Major Overhaul in Chrome Extension Ecosystem!**  
Chrome has started fully phasing out Manifest V2 extensions, including classic ad blockers like uBlock Origin. All previous "workarounds" no longer work. If you rely on older extensions, it's time to quickly find alternatives!  

**💡 AWS Lets AI Tune Its Own Chips**  
AWS launched Neuron Agentic Development, using AI agents to automatically optimize the performance of Trainium/Inferentia chip kernels. No more manual parameter tuning—AI training and inference costs are expected to drop!  

**🇨🇳 MIIT Plans “AI + Communications” Network**  
The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology released a three-year plan focused on building an intelligent computing network system. Nationally driven integration of AI and communication infrastructure will accelerate future computing power network construction.  

**🐰 Meituan Launches an AI Browser!**  
Tabbit 1.0 is officially live, featuring multiple built-in large models that can help automate complex tasks across software and web pages. Key point: the standard version is permanently free! Available now for Windows and macOS.  

**🍎 Apple Critiques “AI for AI’s Sake”**  
Apple executives emphasized at WWDC: useful AI must be user-centered, opposing pure technical showboating. The new Apple Intelligence will focus more on practicality and personalization.  

**🏗️ DeepSeek Starts Building Its Own Data Centers**  
DeepSeek is hiring "IDC Design and Planning Engineers" to build GW-level (gigawatt-scale) data centers in-house. AI companies getting directly involved in infrastructure marks a new phase in the computing power race!  

**⚛️ React Compiler Rewritten in Rust!**  
Meta engineers have merged the PR porting the React Compiler to Rust. Interestingly, the project was largely coded by AI, with humans providing guidance—is this the future of programming?
