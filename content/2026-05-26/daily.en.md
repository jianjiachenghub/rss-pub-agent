## AI

### Amazon Quick Launches Generative Document Features, Cutting Professional Report Writing from Hours to Minutes

**Event:** Amazon Quick released automatic document and visualization generation capabilities, enabling cross-functional professionals to compress document creation time from hours to minutes, shifting work focus from mechanical execution to strategic judgment.

**Why it matters:** Enterprise AI applications have reached a critical inflection point: Amazon Quick has restructured report writing from "data query + manual composition" to "one-click deliverable generation." This means the cost structure of "content production" in knowledge workflows is shifting from labor time to compute consumption. The competitive focus of enterprise software is moving from feature completeness to default output quality, further cementing cloud providers' position as system gateways in the productivity toolchain.
