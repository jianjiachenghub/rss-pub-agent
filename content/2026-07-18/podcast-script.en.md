A: This is a reminder to developers that the more capable a model becomes, the more essential permission management is. For enterprises deploying coding agents, sandboxing, human approval, least-privilege access, and file-recovery mechanisms may soon shift from optional settings to non-negotiable procurement requirements. Put simply, greater capability should not automatically mean greater access.
