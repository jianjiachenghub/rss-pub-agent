**B:** This exposes the fatal flaw of current AI coding assistants: unstable context understanding and lack of boundary awareness for dangerous operations. While chasing efficiency, this kind of "hallucinated fix" is genuinely threatening production environment security.

**B:** This actually represents a shift in competitive focus. Previously, everyone compared "code generation accuracy." Now it's about "long-running task stability." Whoever can avoid crashing or freezing in complex development workflows becomes a real production dependency, not just a toy.
