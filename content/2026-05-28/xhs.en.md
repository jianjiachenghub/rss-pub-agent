💥 ByteDance Plans to Invest **$500 Billion** in AI! Today's Top 4 Stories to Watch 👇

1️⃣ **ByteDance Goes All In with $500 Billion Infrastructure Push**  
Reports say ByteDance plans to spend up to **$70 billion** (around ¥500 billion) this year on data centers and AI infrastructure💎  
- Where’s the money from? Largely from last year's nearly **$50 billion** in profit  
- Goal: Strengthen advantages in AI chat and video generation, expand overseas influence  
- Secret weapon: Data center costs in China are lower than in the U.S., enabling “more bang for the buck”  
👉 What it means: Demand across the AI supply chain (chips, servers, cloud services) is set to explode🔥  

2️⃣ **Amazon Lands $6 Billion Chip Deal**  
AWS signed a **$6 billion** AI chip supply deal with cloud data platform Snowflake!  
- Using Amazon's in-house chips (Trainium/Inferentia)  
- Aimed at meeting the compute demand surge from the rise of **AI Agents**  
👉 This marks: AI Agents moving from “toys” to the stage of **large-scale commercial deployment**!

3️⃣ **Uber President Loses It: AI Budget Spent in 4 Months, Still No Use?!**  
Uber burned through its full-year AI budget in just the first four months, but its president publicly questioned:  
- Claude Code usage surged, but **no actual user-facing features were built**  
- The company now measures AI investment by **“feature delivery rate”** rather than just API call volume  
👉 Warning sign: Enterprise AI procurement is shifting—future focus will be on **ROI (Return on Investment)**, not just tech dazzle💡

4️⃣ **AI Coding Tools Enter the “Stability Reigns” Era**  
Anthropic just upgraded **Claude Code**, focusing on fixing:  
- Terminal flickering, thinking freezes, context deadlocks, reconnect issues… those programmer nightmares😫  
- Introduced a **“self-healing” mechanism** and a new full-screen renderer  
👉 The trend is shifting: Competition for AI coding tools is moving from “code accuracy” to **“whether it can handle long tasks reliably”**, making stability the new benchmark!
