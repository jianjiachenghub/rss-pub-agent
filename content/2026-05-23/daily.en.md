# Personal Daily Report | May 23, 2026

## Today's Take

> Bond markets are flashing warning signals: multiple macro variables are resonating in sync—geopolitical conflicts are elevating inflation expectations, potential new Fed leadership is triggering hawkish repricing, and the ECB is being forced to consider rate hikes to defend credibility. This is no single-event shock, but rather the first concentrated pricing of the "reflation plus tightening" narrative in fixed income. Meanwhile, accelerating AI commercialization and breakthroughs in asset tokenization create a stark contrast: on one side, rising financing costs pressuring the real economy; on the other, liquidity expansion in virtual and intelligent economies. Markets are redrawing risk boundaries.
