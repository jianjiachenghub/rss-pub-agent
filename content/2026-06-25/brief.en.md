**Today's Assessment:** The AI industry is accelerating into a hardcore phase of competing on practical application and infrastructure.

**Key Points:**  
1.  **Intensifying Battle for Power:** Amazon and Google have taken the lead in securing electricity—the "lifeline" of AI computing—highlighting the critical importance of infrastructure.  
2.  **Hardware Demand Validated:** Micron Technology's earnings far exceeded expectations, with its stock surging, providing strong evidence of AI-driven hardware demand.  
3.  **Clear Commercialization Path:** ByteDance's "Doubao" launched three-tier paid subscriptions (up to 500 RMB/month), offering access to more powerful models and office automation capabilities, signaling the start of tiered monetization for large model applications.

**What to Watch Next:** The competitive landscape for key resources like AI chips and power, as well as the paid conversion metrics of leading applications.
