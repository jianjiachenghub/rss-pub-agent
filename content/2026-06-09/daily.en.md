# Personal Daily | June 9, 2026

## Today's Take

> The central thread today is that the AI industry is undergoing a qualitative shift from "technology demonstration" to "infrastructure." Alibaba has established Token Foundry to directly oversee large model operations, OpenAI is removing the "chat" label to reconstruct product forms, and Apple is simultaneously reducing AI costs for developers—the simultaneous adjustments to organizational structure, interaction paradigms, and ecosystem strategies across these three platforms mark the AI industry's entry into a critical phase of engineering deployment and business model validation. This synchronized reconstruction of production relations, product forms, and developer economics carries greater indicative significance for trends than any single technological breakthrough.

## AI

### Alibaba Establishes Token Foundry Division, Wu Yongming Takes Direct Control of Large Model Operations

**Event:** Alibaba announced the merger of the Tongyi Large Model Division and Future Life Laboratory to establish the Token Foundry Division, directly overseen by Group CEO Wu Yongming. Zhou Jingren will serve as Chief Scientist and lead the establishment of Alibaba AI Future Research Institute, with former business lines including Happy Horse and Happy Oyster being integrated into the new department.

**Why it matters:** With large model operations upgraded from an independent division to the CEO-directly-managed Token Foundry, decision-making chains have shortened, meaning model capabilities will directly determine system entry point allocation rights across Alibaba's product ecosystem, with R&D resources concentrating on foundational models and frontier exploration.
