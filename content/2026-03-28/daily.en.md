### Google just gave Android power users a sideloading win

> **Score: 50** | Source: [Hacker News - croemer](https://www.androidauthority.com/android-sideload-carry-over-3652845/)
> Google relaxes Android app sideloading restrictions; user settings can persist across devices

**Event:** Google has adjusted its Android sideloading policy, allowing users to opt out of restrictions on installing apps from unverified developers, with this preference persisting on new devices after a one-time setup.

**Why it matters:** This is a concession by Google under regulatory pressure, effectively weakening Google Play's default distribution power. By ceding security choices to advanced users, Google reduces its exposure to antitrust investigations.
