### 4️⃣ AI Agents: The Crucial Step from “Toy” to “Tool”!
❗️**Key Point**: AI Agents (programs that autonomously handle complex tasks) are hot, but making them reliable, safe, and evaluable is a major challenge.
*   **NVIDIA Tackles Security**: Open-sourced **SkillSpector**, a tool to “check up” on AI Agents for vulnerabilities and malicious behavior.
*   **Amazon Tackles Evaluation**: Open-sourced **Agent-EvalKit** for systematically assessing Agent performance—no more guesswork.
*   **Apache Tackles Engineering**: Incubating the **Burr** project to solve engineering hurdles like state management and tracking for moving Agents from lab to product.
*   **Amazon Lowers the Barrier**: A new feature **automatically optimizes your prompts**, making it easier for business users to extract data from documents with high accuracy using large models.

**📍 Bottom Line**: Leaders are “paving roads and building bridges” in the booming but chaotic Agent space, providing infrastructure. For Agents to be truly useful, they must first be reliable, evaluable, and easy to develop.
