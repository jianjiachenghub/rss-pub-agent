### French Central Bank Pushes Card "Co-Badging" to Weaken Visa and Mastercard Monopoly

**Event:** The French Central Bank is leading the push for "co-badging" payment schemes, mandating that cards issued in France simultaneously support international card organizations (Visa/Mastercard) and domestic clearing networks (like Cartes Bancaires), thereby allowing cardholders and merchants to independently choose clearing paths during transactions, directly challenging international card organizations' market dominance in Europe.

**Why it matters:** Dual-badge models change the allocation of default routing rights in payment networks. Merchant-side system entry shifts from single international card organization monopoly toward multi-network parallel competition, potentially reconstructing rate structures and valuation anchors in the payment industry, weakening the duopoly's exclusive control over transaction data.
