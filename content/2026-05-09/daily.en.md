## AI

### Ai2 Open-Sources EMO: MoE Models Spontaneously Form Modular Structures, Running on Just 12.5% of Experts

**Event:** Ai2 released EMO (Emergent Modularity via pretraining) on Hugging Face, an end-to-end pretrained Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) language model. By constraining all tokens from the same document to route to shared expert subsets, the router spontaneously forms domain-specialized groupings (e.g., medical, code) during training. EMO allows calling just 12.5% of the expert subset to complete specific tasks while maintaining performance close to the full model.

**Why it matters:** Lowers the computational cost threshold for large-model deployment, making "dynamically loading expert subsets by task" a viable product architecture choice, potentially altering the cost structure of cloud inference.
