# Personal Daily | April 22, 2026

## Today's Take

> Technological monopolies and geopolitical risks are converging in a dual squeeze. SpaceX’s acquisition of Cursor and Amazon’s AI shopping dominance reveal rapid consolidation of foundation model capabilities among giants commanding closed loops of data and compute power. Simultaneously, leveraged trading in chip stocks has surged to historic highs, signaling that extreme optimism about AI compute demand has partly front-run future expectations. Regulators are tightening defensively: the Bank of England’s raids on crypto traders and synchronized caution from the ECB and RBI hint that policymakers are reserving policy space for prolonged geopolitical conflict and AI-driven labor displacement.
