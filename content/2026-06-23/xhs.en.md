📈 **Today in Tech, It’s All Explosive News!**

In a nutshell: **AI giants are busy “making money,” “laying off staff,” and “snatching up chips,” while the capital market is both ecstatic and on edge.**

---

### **💥 First: Is AI Starting to Take Jobs?**

**Oracle** today announced layoffs of **21,000 people**. The key point: they stated explicitly that **artificial intelligence has replaced certain roles**. This is the most direct, large-scale official acknowledgment yet of “AI causing unemployment,” and the impact is huge.

### **🤑 Second: Money is Pouring into AI, Unstoppably!**

💰 **MGX** alone raised **$50 billion** for a fund focused on AI, one of the largest ever.
💰 **Menlo Ventures**, an early investor in **Anthropic**, raised $3 billion, fully loaded with ammunition.

This clearly shows: **capital’s bet on AI has reached “crazy” levels**, completely disregarding short-term returns, gambling on the next decade.

### **🔌 Third: The Compute War Escalates. Is NVIDIA Nervous?**

1.  **Amazon** is planning to sell its self-developed **Trainium AI chips** directly to other companies! Previously only selling services on AWS, now they’re selling hardware, **going head-to-head with NVIDIA**.
2.  **Micron** (a memory chip giant) directly **took a stake in Anthropic** and signed a multi-year supply agreement. This is a classic case of **deep binding between AI model companies and chipmakers**.
3.  **Qualcomm** is rumored to be acquiring an AI software company, **Modular**, aiming to shore up its weakness in the AI software ecosystem.

**Interpretation:** The AI compute war has evolved from “buying GPUs” to **self-developing chips, vertical integration, and ecosystem binding**. In the future, if you want to play in AI, you might need some hardware chops just to say hello.

### **🤖 Fourth: AI is “Invading” Your Life**

*   **Claude Becomes a “Colleague”!** Anthropic launched the **Claude Tag** feature on Slack, allowing you to @ it like a coworker, letting it read chat history and plan work. **AI is evolving from a “Q&A machine” to a “team member.”**
*   **ByteDance’s “Doubao” Can Hail Rides!** Piloting in Beijing and Hangzhou, it can directly book CaoCao Chuxing rides via voice command. This means **AI applications are moving from “information services” to “transaction services”**, potentially the prototype of the next super-app.

### **⚠️ Fifth: Risks and Warnings Abound**

*   **Market Correction:** Global tech stocks saw their biggest drop since March. **The hype around AI concept stocks faces its first major test**, with some asking, “Is it overheating?”
*   **Security Warning:** Intelligence agencies of the “Five Eyes” alliance jointly warned that AI-driven cyberattacks and disinformation **could cause major trouble “within months.”**
*   **Big Tech “Speaks Out”:** Most interestingly, **Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella** publicly called to “**break the monopoly of AI giants**,” encouraging the use of cheaper models. This “leader” advocating for antitrust reveals a very subtle strategic game within the ecosystem.
